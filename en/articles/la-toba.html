La Toba

  • Located in the heart of Sierra de Cazorla, Segura y Las Villas Natural Park, La Toba is a small village belonging to Santiago-Pontones.
  • With a population of 40 inhabitants, La Toba gets its name after a stone’s type very common in the area.
  • It is surrounded by water, right next to it, the Segura river pours water into Anchuricas reservoir.
  • The cold, pure and crystal clear water running through the beautiful streets comes from the source of the river inside La Toba’s cave.
  • An unique landscape, the exuberant vegetation. the continuous sound of running water and the range of blues and emerald green of the Anchuricas are waiting for your due visit.
