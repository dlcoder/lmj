Cástulo (2ª Parte)

  • In our second visit to Castulo we check the daily works taking place under Forum MMX’s project.
  • This project, which results have already surpassed all the expectations thanks to some volunteering job, is dedicated to bring back to the surface the Roman Forum of the town.
  • The latest years of findings of great value have taken Castulo’s name along the planet.
  • Probably one of the most relevant ones would be Mosaico de los Amores (Loves Mosaic) - it was considered as one the 10 most important discoveries by the National Geographic Society in 2012- and best preserved; it’s 12 meters long and 6 meters wide, and it shows different scenes representing love, death or seasons.
  • The mosaic is opened to visitors now. It is sheltered under a structure shaped like the original building.
