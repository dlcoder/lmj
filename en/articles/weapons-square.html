Weapons Square

  • Located between La Mella and Jabalcuz, Almodovar hill is well known for its crests that can be seen from any place in the city of Jaen.
  • 992 meters high, this hill is not very high, however it holds very special places for the people from Jaen such as Fuente de la Pena o la Plazoleta de las Armas.
  • Main character in this article, Plazoleta de las Armas is a little flat surrounded by almond trees, hawthorns and pine trees which offers beautiful views of the city.
  • Playing ground, walking area and ‘play hooky’ place for several generations, this place -accessible from La Glorieta neighbourhood- is still a very special place worthy a visit.
